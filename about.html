Our Journey

From a Dream to Chandigarh's Most Loved Studio

Smiling Walls was born in 2019 from a simple belief: every child deserves a celebration as magical as they are. Our founder, Meera Kapoor, a trained artist and mother of two, noticed how stressful birthday planning had become for parents — and how generic the results often felt.

She set out to create something different — a studio where creativity, quality, and genuine joy came first. Starting with just a small team and big dreams, Smiling Walls quickly became the go-to name for birthday parties in Chandigarh.

Today, we've celebrated over 500 birthdays, hosted 1,000+ workshop sessions, and brought smiles to countless faces across Chandigarh, Panchkula, and Mohali.
