What is Resin Art?

Transform Imagination into Liquid Art

Resin art is the craft of creating stunning, glossy pieces by mixing epoxy resin with pigments, glitters, flowers, and other embellishments. The results are breathtakingly beautiful — and completely unique!

At Smiling Walls, our expert instructors guide you through every step of the process, from mixing ratios to pouring techniques. No experience needed — just curiosity and creativity!
